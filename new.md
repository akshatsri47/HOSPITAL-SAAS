# Design Philosophy

Most founders make this mistake:

```text
Homepage = all industries
```

Result:

```text
Confusing
```

Big tech does:

```text
Homepage = Platform

Product Pages = Industries
```

That structure scales from:

```text
4 industries
```

to

```text
40 industries
```

without redesigning the company.

For Xyras, I'd position it as:

> AI Voice Infrastructure for Businesses

and let Healthcare, Real Estate, Restaurants, Automotive, etc. live as solution pages under the Products menu. That gives you a much larger market while still letting you dominate individual verticals.
